## A.4 Reproducibility note (how the corpus was assembled)

1. PDFs were exported from ScienceDirect on **25 Feb 2026** in batches; each batch landed in a timestamped folder under `Dataset/ScienceDirect_articles_25Feb2026_<HH-MM-SS>.<ms>/`.
2. The Part-2 ingestion pipeline (`run_part2.py`, also `Part2_Explicit_Gap_Extraction.ipynb`) loads every `*.pdf` recursively under `Dataset/`, extracts text with `pypdf`, drops articles shorter than 500 words after extraction (treats them as non-articles), and excludes filenames matching `Editorial-Board`.
3. The result is **107 articles → 1,367 sentence-aligned chunks** at the ≤1,000-word boundary (same chunking spirit as GAPMAP's explicit experiments).
4. Each surviving PDF maps 1-to-1 to a `jbee_pdf_NNN` BibTeX key in `Final_Paper/references.bib`. The six editorial-board entries are kept in the `.bib` file for completeness but do not appear in the inventory above and are not cited inline.
